The answer that fooled you

A citation that isn't in the source. A dosage that was invented. A page that doesn't say what the answer claimed — in a document with your name on it.

In law, medicine, and compliance, a confidently wrong answer isn't the model's mistake. It's yours — and you're the one who has to defend it.

What if it just refused?

If it can't cite it, it won't say it.

CiteNexus answers only from retrieved evidence — and abstains when that evidence is weak, missing, or conflicting. The refusal is the feature.

Grounded, verified, or silent

How it holds the line.

Three steps between a question and an answer you can trust. Bring your own LLM and embeddings — CiteNexus owns the grounding.

01 · Ground

Answer from evidence

Temperature-0 answers from retrieved passages only, quoted verbatim in the source language.

02 · Verify

Check every claim

A grounding gate drops any word not in the cited passage. Unsupported claims never reach you.

03 · Cite or abstain

Trace it, or refuse

Every claim resolves to a document, page, and bbox. No support for the question → an honest refusal.

Three verbs, your models

A library, not a black box.

Point it at your documents on S3. Ingest, ask, evaluate — models are injected, orchestration is yours.

quickstart.py
from citenexus import CiteNexus

rag = CiteNexus("s3://my-bucket", signals=["embedding", "text"])

rag.ingest()                        # any document type
answer = rag.ask("Can the employee disclose this?")
score  = rag.evaluate("golden.csv")   # scored + audited
ingest()

What we actually promise

“No ungrounded claim” — not “zero hallucination.”

We won't pretend the model never errs. We guarantee it never asserts what the evidence doesn't support. That honesty is the point — and it's why the core stays small.

It is

  • An evidence-first RAG library you build products on.
  • An ingest engine that resolves every hit to a cited Evidence Unit.
  • Model-agnostic — your embeddings, LLM, reranker, and vision.
  • S3-native, with reproducible, auditable provenance.

It is not

  • Not a model host — nothing bundled; every model is injected.
  • Not a code-comprehension tool — guessed edges betray cite-or-abstain.
  • Not a memory, and not an end-user app — those live on top.
  • Not a promise of zero hallucination — a promise of nothing ungrounded.
